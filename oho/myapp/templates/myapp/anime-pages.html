Attack On Titans

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a gripping action-fantasy anime set in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to gigantic, man-eating Titans.

The story follows Eren Yeager, along with his friends Mikasa and Armin, as they join the Scout Regiment to fight back against the Titans and uncover the dark secrets behind their existence.

Attack on Titan is widely praised for its animation, complex characters, and thought-provoking themes about freedom, survival, and morality.
